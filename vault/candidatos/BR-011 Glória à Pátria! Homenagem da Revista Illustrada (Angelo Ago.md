## Glória à Pátria! Homenagem da Revista Illustrada (Angelo Agostini)

**ID**: BR-011 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 1889-11-16
**Suporte**: Lithograph (periódico ilustrado) | **Instituição**: Biblioteca Nacional
**Arquivo**: Hemeroteca Digital Brasileira

### Descrição

First personified representation of the Republic as a female figure in the Revista Illustrada, published the day after the Proclamation. Central female figure in Greco-Roman dress with Phrygian cap, sword, shield, and Brazilian flag. Alongside Marshal Deodoro da Fonseca on horseback. Considered the inaugural 'Brazilian Marianne' in the illustrated press.
