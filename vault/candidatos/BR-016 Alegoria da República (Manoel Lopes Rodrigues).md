## Alegoria da República (Manoel Lopes Rodrigues)

**ID**: BR-016 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 1896
**Suporte**: Oil on canvas | **Instituição**: Museu de Arte da Bahia
**Arquivo**: dezenovevinte.net

### Descrição

Oil on canvas (230 × 120 cm). Female figure in Greco-Roman dress. White Phrygian cap (usually red) placed on her head by the hand of Providence emerging from a palm tree. Red cloak (allusion to France). Severe expression, non-youthful figure — contrasts with conventional Marianne representations. Commissioned under Prudente de Morais, executed in Paris. Considered an 'exceptional work' and 'poetic license' within the allegorical repertoire.
