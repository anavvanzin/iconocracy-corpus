## La Liberté dictant ses loix aux Nations (Didier)

**ID**: FR-042 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1793–1794
**Suporte**: Engraving (eau-forte, pointillé) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Liberté dictating laws to the Nations. Grandiose composition: Liberté as robed female figure holding the Constitution of 1793, directing the nations, flanked by monuments to Revolutionary martyrs. 34.5 × 27.5 cm. Collection de Vinck.
