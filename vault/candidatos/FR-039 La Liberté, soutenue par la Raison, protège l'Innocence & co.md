## La Liberté, soutenue par la Raison, protège l'Innocence & couronne la Vertu (Boizot / Bernier)

**ID**: FR-039 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1793–1795
**Suporte**: Engraving (eau-forte) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Large-format allegorical print. Liberté (standing female figure with bonnet phrygien and sceptre) supported by Raison (Reason) and protecting Innocence while crowning Vertu (Virtue). Fully developed Revolutionary allegorical program. 36.5 × 53 cm. Ref: Hennin 12177.
