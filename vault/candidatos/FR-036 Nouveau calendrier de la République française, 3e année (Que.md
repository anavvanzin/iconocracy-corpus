## Nouveau calendrier de la République française, 3e année (Queverdo / Picquet)

**ID**: FR-036 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1794
**Suporte**: Engraving (gravure) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Republican calendar for Year III. Rich allegorical composition: female personifications of Justice, Liberté, Égalité, Loi (Law), and Force alongside Revolutionary martyrs (Chalier, Bara, Le Peletier, Marat). Justice holds scales and sword; Liberté holds bonnet phrygien. Hydra of Despotism trampled beneath their feet.
