## Allégorie en l'honneur de Louis XIII — Justice et Prudence (Huret, 1640–1643)

**ID**: FR-053 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1640–1643
**Suporte**: Engraving (burin) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Royal allegorical portrait-frontispiece of Louis XIII surrounded by cardinal/royal virtues: Justice, Prudence, Force, Modération. Companion work to Mazarin allegory (FR-052). Includes reference to the Siege of La Rochelle (1627–28). 33.5 × 22.5 cm.
