## [Les sept vertus] Justicia (Bruegel / Galle, 1559)

**ID**: FR-047 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1559–1560
**Suporte**: Engraving (burin) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

One of the most important Northern Renaissance allegories. Bruegel's 'Justicia' from his series of the Seven Virtues, engraved by Philippe Galle. Justice is a female figure holding sword and scales, surrounded by a courthouse scene of legal proceedings. 25.9 × 32.2 cm. Refs: New Hollstein, Pieter Bruegel the Elder, 16; Lebeer 34.
