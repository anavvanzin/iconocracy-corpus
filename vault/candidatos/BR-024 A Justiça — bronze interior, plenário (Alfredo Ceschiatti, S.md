## A Justiça — bronze interior, plenário (Alfredo Ceschiatti, STF)

**ID**: BR-024 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 1978
**Suporte**: Sculpture (bronze) | **Instituição**: Supremo Tribunal Federal
**Arquivo**: STF Memória

### Descrição

Bronze sculpture of seated goddess Themis (Justiça), blindfolded, at the entrance to the Plenário do Tribunal da Constituição. Second, smaller-scale version of the same iconographic program as the exterior granite work (BR-009). Head detail: 50 × 53 × 65 cm. Damaged in January 8, 2023 attack; rescued from STF gardens during restoration.
