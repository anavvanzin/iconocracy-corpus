## Sept vignettes en-tête de papiers d'administrations (1793)

**ID**: FR-035 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1793
**Suporte**: Engraving (woodcut / eau-forte) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Seven allegorical vignettes from letterheads of Revolutionary-era administrations. All major Republican virtues represented in female allegorical form: Force, Justice, Concorde, Liberté, Espérance, Égalité, République. Collection de Vinck, vol. 28, pièce 4817.
