## O tempo passa, mas as datas gloriosas ficam (Angelo Agostini, Don Quixote)

**ID**: BR-019 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 1895-05-18
**Suporte**: Lithograph (periódico ilustrado) | **Instituição**: Senado Federal — BDSF
**Arquivo**: Biblioteca Digital do Senado Federal

### Descrição

Don Quixote salutes the book of the Lei 13 de Maio while holding a sword to defend the female allegory of the Republic from a snake inscribed 'política glicérica'. Sancho kneels beating the snake. The Republic (allegory) looks at the snake with fear, protected in her cloak. Inverts the combative Marianne role — the Republic as victim/protected figure.
