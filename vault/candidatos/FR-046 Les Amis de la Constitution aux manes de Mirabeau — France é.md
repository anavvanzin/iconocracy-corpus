## Les Amis de la Constitution aux manes de Mirabeau — France éplorée (Lélu, 1791)

**ID**: FR-046 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1791
**Suporte**: Engraving | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France
**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Commemorative allegory of Mirabeau's death. France personified as an 'éplorée' (weeping female figure), joined by Liberté, Renommée (Fame), and Immortalité around the monument to the great orator. Classic funeral/commemorative allegory with female figures.
