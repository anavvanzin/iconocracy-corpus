## La Révolution française — double state avec Justice, Liberté, Égalité (1792–1793)

**ID**: FR-056 | **País**: France | **Data**: 1792–1793
**Suporte**: Engraving (eau-forte coloriée) | **Instituição**: Bibliothèque nationale de France

### Descrição

Large allegorical prints summarizing the French Revolution as a female allegorical program. Multiple female figures representing all Revolutionary virtues. Two states (1792 and 1793) differ in inscriptions. ARKs: btv1b69429317 (1792), btv1b6942930t (1793). A richly documented pair.
