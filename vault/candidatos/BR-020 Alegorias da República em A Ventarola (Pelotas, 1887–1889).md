## Alegorias da República em A Ventarola (Pelotas, 1887–1889)

**ID**: BR-020 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 1887–1889
**Suporte**: Lithograph (periódico ilustrado) | **Instituição**: Biblioteca Pública Pelotense
**Arquivo**: Biblioteca Pública Pelotense (não digitalizado)

### Descrição

Series from the Pelotas illustrated press. (1) 1888: Aged Emperor with heavy crown vs. vigorous young female allegory with Phrygian cap and sword — 'ready for battle'. (2) 1887: Allegory tending a lush plant (Republic) with cap, vs. a dry plant (Empire). Republican woman carries flag inscribed 'porvir' and downward-pointing sword with 'Liberté...'.
