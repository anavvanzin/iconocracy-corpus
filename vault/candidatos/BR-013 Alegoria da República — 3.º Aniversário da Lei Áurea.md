## Alegoria da República — 3.º Aniversário da Lei Áurea

**ID**: BR-013 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 1891-05
**Suporte**: Lithograph (periódico ilustrado) | **Instituição**: Biblioteca Nacional
**Arquivo**: Hemeroteca Digital Brasileira

### Descrição

Female figure of the Republic with Phrygian cap and Brazilian flag on a staff wrapped around her arm. A discharged cannon pointing at 'Lei 13 de maio' inscription. First time the female allegory appears isolated (without the indigenous figure) as the periodical's republican symbol in this context.
