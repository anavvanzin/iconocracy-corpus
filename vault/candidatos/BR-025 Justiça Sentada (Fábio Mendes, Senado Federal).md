## Justiça Sentada (Fábio Mendes, Senado Federal)

**ID**: BR-025 | **País**: Brazil | **Data**: 2018
**Suporte**: Metal relief | **Instituição**: Museu do Senado Federal
**Arquivo**: Tainacan Senado

### Descrição

Allegorical representation of Justice. Seated woman, eyes covered by blindfold, wearing a dress, seated on papers. Right hand: sword with blade pointing down. Left arm: raised, holding scales. All classic Justitia/Themis attributes present (venda, espada, balança). Dimensions: mancha 25.7 × 21.1 cm, obra 29 × 24.2 cm, moldura 40.6 × 35.8 cm. Signed 'Mendes'. Donated to the Senate.
