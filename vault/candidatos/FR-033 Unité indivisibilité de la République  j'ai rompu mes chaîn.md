## Unité indivisibilité de la République : j'ai rompu mes chaînes vive la liberté

**Arquivo**: Gallica

### Descrição

Revolutionary placard combining emblems of the Republic with the motto of liberty. Female allegorical figure of Force/République breaking her chains. Grand format (52 × 39.5 cm). Collection de Vinck, vol. 45, pièce 6131.
