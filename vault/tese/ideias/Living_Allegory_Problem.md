# The Living Allegory Problem

## Core idea

Three items broke the allegory/reality barrier: real women functioning AS allegories. This is a distinct phenomenon deserving its own theoretical treatment.

## Cases

1. **US-008** (Hedwig Reicher, 1913): actress as Columbia in suffrage pageant → BREAKS the contract (women reclaim the allegory)
2. **US-009** (CDV, 1861): anonymous woman as Liberty → REPRODUCES the contract (individual woman consumed by allegory)
3. **UK-006** ("Women of Britain Say Go!", 1915): real women as national voice → INTENSIFIES the contract (real women command real men to die)

## Theoretical question

When a real woman inhabits the allegory, what happens to the Contrato Sexual Visual?

- **It breaks** (Reicher: the allegory-become-flesh demands real rights)
- **It intensifies** (UK-006: real feminine authority deployed for state violence)
- **It dissolves** (CDV: individual body consumed by allegorical function)
