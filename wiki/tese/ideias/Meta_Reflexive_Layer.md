# The Meta-Reflexive Layer — AI Gaze on Allegorical Bodies

## Core idea

This thesis about the gaze on allegorical female bodies is being written with an AI that performed 60 visual analyses of those same bodies in a single day. What is Claude's "gaze"? Is it reproducing the Contrato Sexual Visual by describing/classifying these bodies, or exposing it?

## Questions

- The IconoCode protocol asks the AI to score "dessexualização" — is this itself a form of the male gaze (evaluating how sexual a female body appears)?
- The 10 indicators were designed by a woman (Ana) and applied by an AI — does this change the dynamics of the Contrato Sexual Visual?
- When the AI describes BR-009 as "stripped of all markers of femininity except the most basic bodily form," is it performing the same reduction it diagnoses?

## Methodological note for Cap. 2

Brief reflexive paragraph: the tool that reveals the contract might also participate in it. Acknowledging this is methodological honesty, not self-undermining.
