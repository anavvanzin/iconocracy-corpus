# Déclaration des droits de l'homme et du citoyen (variante)

ID: `cbb9963f-17ae-5163-989a-5ee72f4ba146`

## Descrição
Anonymous engraved broadside variant of the Declaration of the Rights of Man and Citizen (1789). Presents the text of the Declaration within an allegorical decorative framework featuring female figures. Variant edition circulating alongside the canonical Le Barbier design, reflecting the proliferation of allegorical print culture during the Revolution.
