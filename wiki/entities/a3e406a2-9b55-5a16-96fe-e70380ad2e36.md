# Rechtvaardigheid (Justitia)

ID: `a3e406a2-9b55-5a16-96fe-e70380ad2e36`

## Descrição
Engraving of Justice (Rechtvaardigheid / Justitia) by Jacob Matham, 1593. Female allegorical figure in classical dress, holding the sword and scales — canonical attributes of Justitia in Northern European legal iconography of the late 16th century.
