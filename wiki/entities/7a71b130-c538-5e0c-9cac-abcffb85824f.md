# Estátua da Justiça (Luiz Rochet) — referência no Almanak Laemmert

ID: `7a71b130-c538-5e0c-9cac-abcffb85824f`

## Descrição
Reference in the Almanak Laemmert (1929 edition) to the statue of Justice by French sculptor Luiz Rochet, inaugurated September 1872. Alongside statues of History, Poetry and Science. Documents the importation of French allegorical sculpture into Brazilian public space.
