# La Liberté guidant le peuple (Eugène Delacroix)

ID: `ee616189-04ab-525a-9228-d42f774a0557`

## Descrição
'Liberty Leading the People' — the iconic image of the July Revolution of 1830. A bare-breasted female figure (Liberty/Marianne) leads armed citizens over barricades, holding the tricolour flag and a musket. The most powerful visual crystallization of the republic-as-woman — Warner's central case study, Agulhon's key image. Foundational for the entire Iconocracy project.
