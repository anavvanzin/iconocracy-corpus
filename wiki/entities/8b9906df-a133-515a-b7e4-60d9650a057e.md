# Alegoria à História (female allegorical engraving)

ID: `8b9906df-a133-515a-b7e4-60d9650a057e`

## Descrição
Historical allegorical engraving featuring female figures representing History. An early modern Portuguese example of the female allegorical tradition — History personified as a woman, part of the same visual vocabulary that produced Justice and Republic figures.
