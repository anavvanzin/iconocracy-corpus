# Eliseu Visconti — Alegoria (Biblioteca Nacional, Rio de Janeiro)

ID: `9d3c618f-a0d5-5cc2-9091-126fab13fddb`

## Descrição
Impressionist-style allegorical panel for the Presidential Room of the Biblioteca Nacional in Rio de Janeiro. Female allegorical figure in Visconti's distinctive impressionist idiom — the French-trained Brazilian artist adapting European allegorical vocabulary to national institutional decoration. A key example of transatlantic visual transfer.
