# Germania — Reichspost / Deutsches Reich Definitive Stamp (Paul Eduard Waldraff)

ID: `f54bc16b-c0ed-5e46-9fdf-26d55cf26bd2`

## Descrição
Bust of Germania wearing octagonal imperial crown, holding sword and olive branch. Art Nouveau design personally chosen by Kaiser Wilhelm II. Model: actress Anna Führing. Longest-running German definitive series (22 years). NOT used in colonies (replaced by Yacht issue). Overprinted for occupied territories in WWI.
