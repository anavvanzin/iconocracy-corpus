# 5 Francs Hercule — La République et La Justice flanquant Hercule

ID: `b6d12c68-e76d-567a-b7c7-8a4f9756729b`

## Descrição
Hercules standing center flanked by two female allegories: Republic (left, with pike topped by Phrygian cap) and Justice/Equality (right, with triangular level). Design by Dupré (1795), reissued 1848 and 1870. Banned during Second Empire for republican symbols.
