# Germania "Belgien" Overprint — Military Occupation Stamp (WWI)

ID: `99a7c9ba-d743-521e-aef2-73993de09ef7`

## Descrição
Germania definitive stamp overprinted with "Belgien" and revalued in Centimes/Francs for use in German-occupied Belgium during WWI. Same image as DE-GERM-1900 but with additional state inscription marking occupation. First series October 1914, second series May 1916.
