# Women of Britain Say 'Go!'

ID: `970d58c2-fee6-5c70-94e6-50aed8f82cc9`

## Descrição
British WWI recruitment poster by E.J. Kealey (1915), published by the Parliamentary Recruiting Committee (Poster No. 75). Shows two women and a boy at an open window watching soldiers march away. The female figures embody a gendered allegory of domestic Britain urging men to enlist — deploying the moral authority of womanhood as surrogate for national female personification. One of the most iconic British WWI images, widely reproduced and analysed in the history of gender and war propaganda.
