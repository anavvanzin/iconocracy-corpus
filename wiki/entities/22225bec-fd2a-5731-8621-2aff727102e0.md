# Behold! a fabric now to freedom rear'd (Constitutional allegory)

ID: `22225bec-fd2a-5731-8621-2aff727102e0`

## Descrição
Allegorical scene celebrating the US Constitution: Concord standing, Clio writing, Cupid holding the 'Constitution' before a pillar labeled 'Sacred to Liberty Justice and Peace' with thirteen columns. Female allegories deployed to sanctify the constitutional founding.
