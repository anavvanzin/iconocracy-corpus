# Journée du Poilu, 25 et 26 décembre 1915, organisée par le Parlement

ID: `5abac01e-4ee9-5db7-9f8f-a4930e55c54d`

## Descrição
WWI charity poster by Steinlen (1915) for the 'Journée du Poilu' (Soldier's Day, December 25–26), organized by the French Parliament to raise funds for soldiers at the front. The composition depicts a French soldier in the foreground, with a female figure — embodying the Republic/Marianne — in an embrace or gesture of support behind him. One of Steinlen's key contributions to French WWI visual culture, combining social realism with Republican allegorical tradition.
