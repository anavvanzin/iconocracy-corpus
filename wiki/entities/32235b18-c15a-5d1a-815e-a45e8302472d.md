# Lady Justice (F.W. Pomeroy, Old Bailey, London)

ID: `32235b18-c15a-5d1a-815e-a45e8302472d`

## Descrição
The iconic gold statue of Lady Justice atop the Old Bailey, London's Central Criminal Court. Notably unblindfolded — holding sword and scales, with spiked crown. The courthouse brochures explain that 'her maidenly form is supposed to guarantee her impartiality.' A prime example of how the female body itself is argued to embody legal virtue.
