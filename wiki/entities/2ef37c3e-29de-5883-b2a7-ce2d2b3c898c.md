# Gerechtigkeit (Allegory of Justice) by Lucas Cranach the Elder

ID: `2ef37c3e-29de-5883-b2a7-ce2d2b3c898c`

## Descrição
Painting showing the personification of Justice as a half-length nude female figure against dark background, holding sword and scales. Inscribed 'GERECHTIGKAEIT'. One of the most influential Renaissance depictions — Cranach's eroticized Justice figure set a visual template that persisted for centuries. Northern Renaissance.
