# Detalhe do monumento à República, Praça da República, Belém

ID: `d5c61575-cba7-5262-bf20-9017fad5d3cd`

## Descrição
Close-up detail of female allegorical figure holding a flag, standing on a lion. Part of the Monument to the Republic in Praça da República, Belém. The female figure personifies the Brazilian Republic with classical republican iconography — flag, lion (strength), classical garb.
