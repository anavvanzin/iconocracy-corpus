# Rechtvaardigheid (Justitia) / Iustitia

ID: `59156365-09f3-5165-bdbb-f0f936fa4c27`

## Descrição
Anonymous engraving of Justice (Iustitia) after the design tradition of Hendrick Goltzius and Jacob Matham. Female allegorical figure representing Justitia with the traditional attributes of sword and scales. Undated impression from the period 1585–1637.
