# Temple of Liberty

ID: `d349a40c-aedc-533f-9e16-6ea7bbc1cc8e`

## Descrição
Woodcut broadside by Jared W. Bell (1834) depicting the Temple of Liberty as an allegorical female figure — the Goddess of Liberty — enthroned or standing within a classical temple. A key example of antebellum American female allegorical imagery deploying classical architecture and female personification to represent republican values and national identity.
