# Allégorie de la Justice (Joseph Stallaert, Brussels City Hall)

ID: `8d68ddd2-0db5-571f-924a-fd2e6de46623`

## Descrição
Allegorical painting of Justice in the Mayor's Cabinet of Brussels City Hall, by Joseph Stallaert. Part of a triptych with Charity and Foresight — three female allegorical virtues required of a mayor. Justice symbolized by scales and sword. A Belgian civic example connecting female virtue to political authority.
