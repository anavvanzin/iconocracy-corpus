# Relief: Gerechtigkeit und Eintracht (Bremen Rathaus)

ID: `5aa4b8a3-717f-5893-af0e-a0e6641fd3ff`

## Descrição
Relief of Justice (Gerechtigkeit) and Concord (Eintracht) on the Bremen Old Town Hall (1596–1605). Female Justice paired with female Concord on a civic building — the visual argument that justice produces social harmony.
