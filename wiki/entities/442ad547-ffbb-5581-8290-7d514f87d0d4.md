# Le hochet de la République (Jean Veber)

ID: `442ad547-ffbb-5581-8290-7d514f87d0d4`

## Descrição
Satirical print 'The Rattle of the Republic' by Jean Veber. The Republic as a woman reduced to a toy or rattle — a critical/ironic commentary on the infantilization or trivialization of republican symbolism through its female form.
