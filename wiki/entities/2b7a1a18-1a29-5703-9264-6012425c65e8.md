# République Française: 3e Emprunt de la Défense nationale

ID: `2b7a1a18-1a29-5703-9264-6012425c65e8`

## Descrição
WWI propaganda poster for the 3rd National Defense loan. Marianne stands triumphantly above soldiers, embodying the Republic rallying her citizens to war. The female allegory mobilized for military propaganda — a critical junction of gender, state power, and visual persuasion.
