# La République aimable (Félicien Rops)

ID: `eac80e8f-9794-5610-8a38-962119d0cd14`

## Descrição
Satirical engraving by Belgian-French artist Félicien Rops depicting 'The Amiable Republic' — a subversive or eroticized representation of the Republic as a woman. Critical for understanding the contested gendered nature of republican allegory and its use in satirical press.
