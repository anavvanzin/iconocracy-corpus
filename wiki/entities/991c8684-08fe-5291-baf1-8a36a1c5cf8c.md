# A Republica precisa de ti: vota em Norton de Matos

ID: `991c8684-08fe-5291-baf1-8a36a1c5cf8c`

## Descrição
Electoral poster from the 1949 Portuguese presidential campaign featuring a female allegorical figure representing the Portuguese Republic. 'The Republic needs you: vote for Norton de Matos.' A classical female figure in robes symbolizes the Republic during a period of authoritarian governance — the allegory deployed as opposition to dictatorship.
