# Palais de Justice de Bruxelles (Joseph Poelaert)

## Descrição
The Brussels Palace of Justice — one of the largest courthouses ever built and a monument to 19th-century legal grandeur. The 'pompous staircase with sculptures attired in Roman robes' and allegorical female figures throughout. Poelaert's design made the Belgian state visible through classical feminine allegory. Central to the dissertation's Belgian case study and Huygebaert's colonial paternalism analysis.
