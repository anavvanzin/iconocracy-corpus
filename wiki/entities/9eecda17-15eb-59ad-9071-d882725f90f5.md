# A Justiça (Alfredo Ceschiatti, STF Brasília)

ID: `9eecda17-15eb-59ad-9071-d882725f90f5`

## Descrição
Monumental granite sculpture of Justice (Themis) seated before the Supreme Federal Tribunal in Brasília. Eyes closed, holding a sword with both hands. Designed to complement Oscar Niemeyer's modernist architecture. The Brazilian Justiça — a seated, blindfolded, sword-bearing woman as the face of the highest court. Attacked on January 8, 2023, making it a central object in the dissertation's analysis of iconoclasm and legal literacy.
