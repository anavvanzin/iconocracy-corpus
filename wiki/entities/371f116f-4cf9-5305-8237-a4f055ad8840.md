# Allégorie pour servir de frontispice au compte-rendu au Roi par M. Necker

ID: `371f116f-4cf9-5305-8237-a4f055ad8840`

## Descrição
Third allegorical engraving serving as frontispiece for Necker's 'Compte rendu au Roi' (1781). This variant reinforces the corpus of female allegorical imagery mobilized around the question of royal fiscal accountability in the late Ancien Régime. The three Necker frontispieces (FR-015, FR-016, FR-017) together document the visual politics of the transparency debate.
