# Alegoria ao Tempo (Pierre Antoine Quillard)

ID: `b3ea808a-fabc-5f0d-975f-81249e3f741a`

## Descrição
Allegorical engraving representing Time, companion piece to the Alegoria à Morte. Part of Quillard's allegorical series produced in Lisbon. Time as a personified figure — part of the broader iconographic programme where abstract concepts (Justice, Time, Truth, Death) take human form.
