# Alegoria à Morte (Pierre Antoine Quillard)

ID: `b83229c4-0c92-5c0d-8df4-590e246bc7a8`

## Descrição
Allegorical engraving representing Death, part of a series of allegorical works by the French-Portuguese artist Quillard produced in Lisbon. Demonstrates the French-Portuguese artistic exchange in allegorical visual culture during the Baroque period.
