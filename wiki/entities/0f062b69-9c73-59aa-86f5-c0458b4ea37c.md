# [Liberté, Égalité, Fraternité — portrait allégorique d'Ernouf]

ID: `0f062b69-9c73-59aa-86f5-c0458b4ea37c`

## Descrição
Revolutionary allegorical print combining the motto Liberté, Égalité, Fraternité with a portrait of Ernouf. Designed by Garneray and engraved by Quéverdo, this composite image exemplifies the visual culture of the Directory period, deploying female allegorical figures to embody Republican values alongside the honored portrait. Documents the consolidation of Marianne iconography in post-Terror Republican print culture.
