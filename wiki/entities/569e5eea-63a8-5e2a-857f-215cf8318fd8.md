# Justitia (marble head from Porta di Capua)

ID: `569e5eea-63a8-5e2a-857f-215cf8318fd8`

## Descrição
Marble head of Justitia from the Porta di Capua, built under Emperor Frederick II. One of the earliest surviving medieval personifications of Justice as a woman — predating the Renaissance tradition. Connected to the Hohenstaufen revival of Roman legal imagery.
