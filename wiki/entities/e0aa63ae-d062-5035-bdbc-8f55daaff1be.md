# Allegorie der Jus Civile (Christian Wenzinger)

ID: `e0aa63ae-d062-5035-bdbc-8f55daaff1be`

## Descrição
Allegory of Civil Law (Jus Civile) by Christian Wenzinger, 1752, at a former Benedictine abbey. Distinction between civil and canonical law personified — Justice in her specifically secular legal form within an ecclesiastical setting.
