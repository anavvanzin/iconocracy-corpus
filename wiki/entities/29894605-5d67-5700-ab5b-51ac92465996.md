# Déclaration des droits de l'homme et du citoyen

ID: `29894605-5d67-5700-ab5b-51ac92465996`

## Descrição
Engraved broadside of the Declaration of the Rights of Man and Citizen (1789), designed by Le Barbier l'aîné and engraved by Laurent and Dien. The composition frames the text of the Declaration with two female allegorical figures — Liberty with Phrygian cap and Justice with scales — flanking a radiating triangle (Eye of Providence). Canonical image of Revolutionary visual rhetoric fusing law, allegory, and print.
