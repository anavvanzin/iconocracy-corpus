# La Liberté, soutenue par la Raison, protège l'Innocence et couronne la Vertu

ID: `dd101f3a-1ebe-5fcb-b337-f678521f39d1`

## Descrição
Multi-figure allegorical composition. Winged Liberty at left crowns Virtue (seated right, holding radiant mirror/sun). Reason at center steadies the scene. Innocence crouches at Virtue's feet. Lion at far right. Five female figures in classical drapery arranged in a sculptural relief composition. Inscription below: 'La Liberté, soutenue par la Raison, protège l'Innocence et couronne la Vertu'. After a design by Boizot.
