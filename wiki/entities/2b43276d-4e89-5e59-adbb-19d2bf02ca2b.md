# Gerechtigkeitsbrunnen mit Plastik der Justitia (Hans Gieng, 1543)

ID: `2b43276d-4e89-5e59-adbb-19d2bf02ca2b`

## Descrição
The Gerechtigkeitsbrunnen (Fountain of Justice) in Bern — the first known blindfolded representation of Lady Justice (1543). Hans Gieng's statue holds sword and scales, blindfolded, standing atop vanquished figures representing worldly power (pope, sultan, emperor, magistrate). A foundational image in the history of legal iconography.
