# Wake up America! Civilization calls every man, woman and child!

ID: `39ebfe77-0d0b-5130-8cce-f6f48d85a081`

## Descrição
WWI recruitment poster by James Montgomery Flagg (1917) depicting Columbia — the female personification of America — sleeping, dressed in patriotic stars and stripes and wearing a Phrygian cap. The figure of Columbia, modeled on actress Mary Arthur, embodies the nation's allegorical identity. One of the most iconic American female allegorical images of the First World War, later reproduced as one of the most widely distributed WWI posters. Published for 'Wake Up, America' Day, April 19, 1917.
