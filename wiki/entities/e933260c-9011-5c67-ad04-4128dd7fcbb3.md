# Liberté (d'après Moitte)

ID: `e933260c-9011-5c67-ad04-4128dd7fcbb3`

## Descrição
Seated female Liberty holding a Phrygian cap aloft on a pole in her right hand. Classical drapery, laurel crown. Seated on a stone pedestal inscribed 'LIBERTÉ'. Left arm rests on fasces bound with ribbons. Serpent crushed beneath the pedestal. Dark background emphasizing the sculptural white figure. After a design by sculptor Moitte.
