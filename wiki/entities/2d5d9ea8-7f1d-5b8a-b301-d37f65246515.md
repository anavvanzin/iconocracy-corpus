# Rechtvaardigheid (Justitia)

ID: `2d5d9ea8-7f1d-5b8a-b301-d37f65246515`

## Descrição
Engraving of Justice (Justitia) as a female allegorical figure, made by Jacob Matham after Hendrick Goltzius in 1597. Part of the series depicting the Four Cardinal Virtues. Justitia stands with sword and scales.
