# Allégorie pour le frontispice du compte-rendu au Roi par M. Necker

ID: `b84f6d98-c8c0-58ce-b923-260059ec06ab`

## Descrição
Engraved allegorical frontispiece designed for Necker's 'Compte rendu au Roi' (1781), the public financial report that became a landmark in Ancien Régime transparency. Female allegorical figures — variously interpreted as Justice, Abundance, and Truth — frame the royal accounting. An early instance of female allegory deployed in the visual rhetoric of fiscal governance.
