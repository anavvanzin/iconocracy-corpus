# 5 Francs — 50 ans de Belgique: La Belgique avec Constitution et Lion

## Descrição
Commemorative coin for 50th anniversary of Belgian independence. Reverse: personification of Belgium leaning on lion, holding Belgian Constitution. Congress Column to left, Brussels Palace of Justice to right. Obverse: jugate busts of Leopold I and Leopold II.
