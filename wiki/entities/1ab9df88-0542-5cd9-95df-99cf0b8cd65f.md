# Justiça popular (UDP political poster)

ID: `1ab9df88-0542-5cd9-95df-99cf0b8cd65f`

## Descrição
Political poster from the Portuguese revolutionary period (post-Carnation Revolution, 1974): 'Popular Justice: the PIDE agent who murdered Dias Coelho will be judged at the people's cost!' Justice invoked in revolutionary political context — a moment when the concept of Justice was being radically renegotiated.
