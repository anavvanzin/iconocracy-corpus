# Justice and History (plaster model for US Capitol)

ID: `416a2c38-c975-5121-aa28-33166a03a0cf`

## Descrição
Photograph of plaster model for 'Justice and History' by Thomas Crawford, designed for the Senate bronze doors at the East Portico of the US Capitol. Justice and History as paired female figures guarding the entrance to the legislative chamber.
