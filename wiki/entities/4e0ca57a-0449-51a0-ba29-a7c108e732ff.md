# Semeuse — Selo Definitivo da República Francesa (Oscar Roty / Mouchon)

ID: `4e0ca57a-0449-51a0-ba29-a7c108e732ff`

## Descrição
Marianne in flowing robes and Phrygian cap sowing seeds with extended right arm, seed bag under left arm. Rising sun on horizon (1903 version). Design from Roty medal (1887), adapted for coin (1897) then stamp (1903). Quintessential NORMATIVE Marianne: peaceful, productive, nurturing.
