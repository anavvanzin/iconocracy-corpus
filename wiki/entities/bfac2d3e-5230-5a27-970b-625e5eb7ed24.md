# Alegoria da República (Décio Villares)

ID: `bfac2d3e-5230-5a27-970b-625e5eb7ed24`

## Descrição
Allegorical painting of the Brazilian Republic by Décio Villares, the positivist artist who designed the blue disc on Brazil's national flag. The female figure wears a Phrygian cap inscribed with 'Ordem e Progresso' — directly linking republican feminine allegory to positivist ideology. Key work for understanding how European allegorical forms were transplanted and adapted in Brazilian republican iconography.
