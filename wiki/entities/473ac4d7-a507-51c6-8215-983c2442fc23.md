# Columbia calls—Enlist now for U.S. Army

ID: `473ac4d7-a507-51c6-8215-983c2442fc23`

## Descrição
WWI recruitment poster (1917) depicting Columbia standing on the globe atop North America, holding the American flag and a sword, calling men to enlist. Designed by Frances Adams Halsted (who also wrote the accompanying poem) and painted by Vincente Aderente. After the U.S. entered WWI on April 6, 1917, Halsted donated the design to the War Department; the New York Times planned 500,000 copies. A canonical example of Columbia as female national allegory in wartime American visual culture.
