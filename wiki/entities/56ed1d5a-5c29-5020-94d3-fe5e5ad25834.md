# Calendrier républicain (An III)

ID: `56ed1d5a-5c29-5020-94d3-fe5e5ad25834`

## Descrição
Republican calendar (Year III) in two engraved panels. Upper register features female allegorical figures flanking a central medallion with fasces and Phrygian cap. Left panel: Liberty and Equality with civic attributes; right panel: agricultural scene under arched pediment. Columns of calendar months below in republican nomenclature. Decorative border with diamond patterns and medallions.
