# [Alegoría de la República española y la República francesa]

ID: `7fdb154e-59f3-50ca-bacf-c3bf48f088d9`

## Descrição
Allegorical drawing or print depicting the Spanish Republic and the French Republic as paired female figures. Produced in the context of the proclamation of the Second Spanish Republic (April 14, 1931), evoking the fraternal bond between the two republican nations. Part of the BNE's collection of drawings, engravings, and photographs relating to the Second Republic. The Spanish Republic figure wears the Phrygian cap and holds attributes of justice and liberty, mirroring the Marianne iconotype of France.
