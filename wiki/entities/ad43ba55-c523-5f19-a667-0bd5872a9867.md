# Alegoria da República (Carlos Chambelland)

ID: `ad43ba55-c523-5f19-a667-0bd5872a9867`

## Descrição
Allegorical painting commissioned for Brazil's centennial of independence (1922). Female figure in flowing white and green robes, arm raised, accompanied by an angel, with male figures holding manuscripts below — blending republican allegory with national celebration.
