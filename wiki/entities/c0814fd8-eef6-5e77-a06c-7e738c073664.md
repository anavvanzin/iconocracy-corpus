# 100 Francs Banque du Congo Belge — Allegory of Europe Kneeling with African Boys

ID: `c0814fd8-eef6-5e77-a06c-7e738c073664`

## Descrição
European woman kneeling, holding fabric. Two African boys — one seated, one standing holding medallion with profile of crowned European woman. Elephant tusks and exotic fruits on the ground. Paternalistic colonial imagery: the European woman 'civilizes' through textile/clothing. The medallion creates a mise en abyme: the colonized subject holds the image of the sovereign.
