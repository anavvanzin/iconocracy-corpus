# Iustitia (d'après Bruegel)

ID: `4e83a07f-ca7d-53c6-a77d-500ce3ec88ce`

## Descrição
Panoramic allegorical engraving of Justice (Iustitia) from Bruegel's Seven Virtues series. Central female figure of Justice stands blindfolded with sword and scales, surrounded by scenes of juridical punishment: scaffolds, flogging, public execution, interrogation, courtroom proceedings. Latin inscription: 'SCOPVS LEGIS EST AVT VT EVQVE PVNIT EMENDET, AVT POENA EIVS CAETEROS MELIORES REDDAT AVT SVBLATIS MALIS CAETERI SECVRIORES VIVANT'.
