# Illustrations de Calendrier et compost des bergiers (Danse macabre — female Justice figure)

ID: `0d805e40-ced2-5531-827d-4584ae6afc55`

## Descrição
Illustrations from the 'Calendar and Almanac of Shepherds' featuring the dance of death and allegorical figures including Justice. An early representation of Justice as a female figure within the memento mori tradition — the intersection of death, morality, and law.
