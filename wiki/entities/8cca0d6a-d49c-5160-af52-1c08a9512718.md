# Rechtvaardigheid (Justitia)

ID: `8cca0d6a-d49c-5160-af52-1c08a9512718`

## Descrição
Engraving depicting Justice (Justitia) as a female allegorical figure, attributed to Jacob Matham after a design by Hendrick Goltzius. Part of a series on the virtues. Justitia shown standing, holding a sword and scales, blindfolded.
