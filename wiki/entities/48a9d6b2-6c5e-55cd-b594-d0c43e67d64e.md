# Female Allegory (Pierre Puvis de Chavannes)

ID: `48a9d6b2-6c5e-55cd-b594-d0c43e67d64e`

## Descrição
Female allegorical figure by Pierre Puvis de Chavannes, the great French muralist whose public commissions (Sorbonne, Panthéon, Hôtel de Ville) defined the visual language of Third Republic civic spaces. This drawing connects to the larger programme of republican allegorical decoration.
