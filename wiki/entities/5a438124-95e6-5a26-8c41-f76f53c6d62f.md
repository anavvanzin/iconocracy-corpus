# Woman holding sword and scales of justice (Jurisprudentia)

ID: `5a438124-95e6-5a26-8c41-f76f53c6d62f`

## Descrição
Engraving by Étienne Delaune (c. 1560) depicting a female figure holding a sword and scales — the canonical attributes of Justice — identified as Jurisprudentia. Delaune was a French goldsmith and engraver active in mid-16th century France and Germany; this work, held at the Wellcome Collection, exemplifies Renaissance allegorical personification of law and jurisprudence.
