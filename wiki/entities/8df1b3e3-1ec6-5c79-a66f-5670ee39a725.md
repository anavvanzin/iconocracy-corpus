# Allegory of Justice (Charles Audran after B.P. Cortona)

ID: `8df1b3e3-1ec6-5c79-a66f-5670ee39a725`

## Descrição
Allegorical representation of Justice, engraved by Charles Audran after an invention by Pietro da Cortona (Berrettini). Italian Baroque model of Justice as a female figure — one of the visual templates that circulated across Europe and eventually reached the Americas.
