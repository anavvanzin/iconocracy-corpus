# Justitia civilis (Maarten de Vos)

ID: `ece3eab6-a3f1-5305-a03f-ae92d81c2df0`

## Descrição
Allegorical depiction of civil justice (Justitia civilis) by Flemish master Maarten de Vos. Distinction between civil and criminal justice personified as separate female figures — important for understanding the multiplication of legal feminine allegories.
