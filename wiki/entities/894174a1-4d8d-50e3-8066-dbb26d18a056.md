# La République nous appelle... (Steinlen)

ID: `894174a1-4d8d-50e3-8066-dbb26d18a056`

## Descrição
WWI recruitment poster: 'The Republic calls us...' — Marianne as a summoning female figure, calling citizens to arms. Steinlen's powerful composition shows the Republic-as-woman as a quasi-maternal/military figure demanding sacrifice.
