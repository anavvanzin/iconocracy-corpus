# Alegoria da República (Estados Unidos do Brasil)

ID: `8db149b7-2f6e-5f2b-9f94-7163e2633cbb`

## Descrição
Seated female allegory of the Brazilian Republic enthroned. Wears white classical dress with green mantle (national colors), laurel crown with red Phrygian cap, golden necklace. Right hand rests on a sword, left hand on the throne's armrest with lion-head finials. Palm frond at her feet. Background mosaic inscription 'ESTADOS UNIDOS DO BRASIL' with Roman numeral MDCCCLXXXIX (1889, date of Republic proclamation). Ornamental border with blue rosettes.
