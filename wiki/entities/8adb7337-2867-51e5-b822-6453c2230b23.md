# Allégorie pour le frontispice du compte rendu au Roi par M. Necker (variante)

ID: `8adb7337-2867-51e5-b822-6453c2230b23`

## Descrição
Second variant of the allegorical engraving designed as frontispiece for Necker's 'Compte rendu au Roi' (1781). Female allegorical figures in a variant compositional arrangement. Documents the multiple print versions in circulation around this seminal text on royal finances and public accountability.
