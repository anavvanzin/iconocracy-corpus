# The Blessings of Victory — Liberty with Sword and Columbia with Palm Branch (Thomas Nast)

ID: `3554f7c3-1f9f-5f5a-9211-b085bf99c08d`

## Descrição
From Harper's Weekly, September 24, 1864. Liberty holding sword, Columbia holding palm branch, caption 'Victory will bring us Peace.' Vignettes: prisoners of war returning home, soldier embracing wife, veteran's welcome. Two female allegories differentiated: Liberty (armed/militant) vs. Columbia (pacific/maternal). Thomas Nast — same artist who created the Republican elephant and Democratic donkey.
