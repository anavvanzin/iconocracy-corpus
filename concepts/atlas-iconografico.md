# Atlas Iconográfico

A synthetic component of the Iconocracia research, this Atlas organizes and visualizes the findings from the quantitative (Iconometria) and qualitative (Iconologia) analyses. It aims to map the relationships between different iconographic elements, historical periods, and conceptual frameworks.
