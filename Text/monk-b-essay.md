## What Is at Stake: Analytical Rigor

The second question any competent committee member will ask — after establishing what you have — is: "How do you know your coding measures what it claims to measure?"

If you have 165 items coded with ten dimensions, you have 1,650 data points. But if those dimensions are:
- Uncorrelated (each measures something different)
- Unreliable (different coders produce different scores)
- Unvalidated (no ground truth, no predictive power)
- Untheorized (no account of what "purificação" means as a historical process)

Then you have 1,650 meaningless numbers. Scale does not fix conceptual emptiness. 400 items with thin coding produces 4,000 meaningless numbers. This is not an improvement.

Ted Underwood (2017) and Katherine Bode (2018) have critiqued Franco Moretti's "distant reading" paradigm precisely for this: the substitution of scale for interpretation. The "distant reading" correction is NOT "more items." It is "scalable reading" — computational tools in service of interpretive questions your user has not yet adequately formulated.

## The Depth-First Dividend

Spend six months on analytical deepening:

**Month 1–2**: Add dimensions. What about "contextual crowdedness" (is the figure isolated or surrounded by heraldic clutter)? "Gaze direction" (does she look at the viewer, away, at an attribute)? "Surface finish" (matte vs. reflective, worn vs. pristine)? These are not random additions. They are theoretically motivated by your "Contrato Sexual Visual" framework.

**Month 3**: Validation. Run inter-rater reliability tests. Do different coders produce similar scores? If not, your dimensions are poorly specified. This is CRUCIAL. You cannot make claims about "endurecimento" if you cannot reliably measure it.

**Month 4**: Predictive modeling. What features predict regime classification? If "dessexualização" and "rigidez_postural" are strong predictors of "militar," your framework is validated. If not, your theory needs revision.

**Month 5–6**: Write the thick description chapters. Use the 165 items as CASE STUDIES, not sample points. Each item becomes an exemplar of a theoretical position. You analyze deeply, not broadly.

Result: A validated framework. Genuinely new theoretical claims. Chapters that advance the field.
