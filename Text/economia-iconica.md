# Economia Icônica

A concept developed by [[Mondzain]] (2002),[[¹]] which analyzes the circulation and value of images within a given society, framing them as a form of economic currency.

In the context of Iconocracia, this concept helps understand how allegorical representations of the female form accrue symbolic and political value.
